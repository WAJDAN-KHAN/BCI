Corporate Responsibility

Responsibility, values and social accountability start at the top at Blackstone

We have business practices that involve participating in initiatives that benefit our society. Sustainability is embedded into the core of our operations to create positive value for business and society. Blackstone has and will continue to be committed to reducing our environmental impacts and contributing positively to the community.

Blackstone is a sustainable company that works to protect the environment by conserving, recycling, reducing and reusing resources whenever possible. Our practices are conducted in a socially responsible and ethical manner.

We are committed to giving back, improving our environment and staying involved in our community

Blackstone donates, sponsors events, hosts luncheons, and volunteers for multiple non-profits in the Los Angeles area to include the J3Foundation Los Angeles, C5 Los Angeles, Habitat for Humanity, Baby2Baby, Los Angeles Food Bank and the Salvation Army.
