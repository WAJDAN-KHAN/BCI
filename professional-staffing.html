Professional Staffing

Facilitating meaningful connections between businesses and individuals

At Alakey Consulting, we specialize in full life cycle staff augmentation services, emphasizing top-tier consultants, strong client relationships, and exceptional consultant customer service. Leveraging our extensive network and dynamic database of qualified professionals, we offer expertise across administrative, technical, and professional fields. We recognize that every employee significantly influences your company's quality and resilience.

Our Process

  • Attracting and identfying qualified candidates
  • Prescreen interview prior to being sent to client
  • Zoom/Web-based interview if the candidate is not local
  • Reference checks (peer and professional)
  • Background/Drug Screen
  • First Day Check
  • Week 1 round-up
  • Bi-monthly quality checks
  • Performance review after 6 months of service (with assistance from the client)
  • Exit interview
  • Reentry into the workforce
